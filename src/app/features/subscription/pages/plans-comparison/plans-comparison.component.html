Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our plans

Can I change my plan later?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade at any time. Changes take effect immediately for upgrades, and at the end of the current billing period for downgrades. We'll automatically prorate any charges.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Visa, Mastercard, and American Express via Stripe. For users in Africa, we also support Paystack for local card and mobile money payments. PayPal is available for credit pack purchases.

Is there a free trial?

Yes! All paid plans come with a 14-day free trial. No credit card is required to start. You can explore all features risk-free and decide which plan works best for you.

What happens when I reach my limits?

You'll receive notifications at 80% and 100% of your limits. You can purchase credit packs for additional OCR pages without changing your plan, or upgrade anytime for higher limits.

Can I cancel anytime?

Absolutely. There are no long-term contracts. Cancel anytime from your account settings. Your access continues until the end of your current billing period, and we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee.

Do you support multiple currencies?

Yes! We support 64+ currencies with real-time exchange rate conversion. Prices automatically display in your local currency. All base pricing is in USD with transparent conversion rates.
